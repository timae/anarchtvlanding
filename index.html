Everything else

Built for the real-world messiness of IPTV.

Every edge case that plagues other players — handled.

⚙️

Dual Player Engine

VLC is the default on iOS and Apple TV; AVPlayerView on macOS. Switch per stream in Settings. Up to 5 auto-retries with exponential backoff.

🔄

Smart Reconnect

Exponential backoff with up to 5 automatic retries. A "Retry Now" CTA appears so you don't have to leave the player to restart.

📺

Picture in Picture

Keep watching while you browse. PiP works system-wide so you never miss a moment.

🎙️

Audio Track Selection

Multi-audio streams let you pick your language. Tap the waveform icon in the player controls to switch tracks mid-stream.

⭐️

Favourites

Star channels for instant access. Favourites appear at the top of the Live TV tab across every session.

🔒

Multiple Sources

Add M3U playlists or Xtream Codes accounts. Passwords stored in the system Keychain. Per-source refresh interval: manual, 6h, 12h, daily, or weekly.

🔔

Watch Alerts

Define keywords (e.g. "Champions League") and get a push notification whenever a matching live event appears in your EPG — so you never miss it.

⏮️

Timeshift DVR Coming Soon

Pause, rewind, and scrub back through live TV on Apple TV. Recording to a local buffer — in the works.

🎬

Overseerr Integration

Connect your Overseerr or Jellyseerr server to request movies and shows directly from the AnarchTV Library — without switching apps.

Privacy

Your data stays on your device.

AnarchTV is designed with privacy as a default, not an afterthought.

🚫

No tracking

No analytics, no crash reporters, no third-party SDKs that phone home. Ever.

📱

Local database

Your channel list, watch history, and favourites live only in a local SQLite database on your device.

🔑

Keychain for passwords

Xtream Codes passwords are stored in the system Keychain. They are never sent to any server other than your IPTV provider.

🌐

Outbound connections

The app only connects to your IPTV source, TMDB (for trending posters, optional), and Apple's AirPlay infrastructure.
