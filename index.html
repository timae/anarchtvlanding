Built for the real-world messiness of IPTV.

Every edge case that plagues other players — handled.

⚙️

Dual Player Engine

Switch between Apple AVKit and the VLC backend per stream. Auto mode picks VLC for 4K / UHD titles, AVKit for everything else.

🔄

Smart Reconnect

Exponential backoff with up to 5 automatic retries. A "Retry Now" CTA appears so you don't have to leave the player to restart.

📺

Picture in Picture

Keep watching while you browse. PiP works system-wide so you never miss a moment.

🎙️

Audio Track Selection

Multi-audio streams let you pick your language. Tap the waveform icon in the player controls to switch tracks mid-stream.

⭐️

Favourites

Star channels for instant access. Favourites appear at the top of the Live TV tab across every session.

🔒

Multiple Sources

Add M3U playlists or Xtream Codes accounts. Passwords stored securely in the iOS Keychain — never in plain text.

Get started

Up and running in minutes.

1

Download AnarchTV

Install free from the App Store on any iPhone or iPad running iOS 17 or later.

2

Add your source

Paste an M3U URL or enter your Xtream Codes host, username, and password in Settings → Add Source.

3

Sync your library

AnarchTV imports channels, movies, and series in the background. Large libraries (50,000+ items) complete in under a minute.

4

Watch

Browse live TV, search your library, check the guide, and start watching — no account, no subscription, no ads.

Privacy

Your data stays on your device.

AnarchTV is designed with privacy as a default, not an afterthought.

🚫

No tracking

No analytics, no crash reporters, no third-party SDKs that phone home. Ever.

📱

Local database

Your channel list, watch history, and favourites live only in a local SQLite database on your device.

🔑

Keychain for passwords

Xtream Codes passwords are stored in the iOS Keychain. They are never sent to any server other than your IPTV provider.

🌐

Outbound connections

The app only connects to your IPTV source, TMDB (for trending posters, optional), and Apple's AirPlay infrastructure.
